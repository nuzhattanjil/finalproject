Coffee shops I have visited in the past year!



Milk and Pull



Location: 348 Malcolm X. Blvd, Brooklyn, NY 11233


What I ordered - Earl Grey Latte and Pistachio Croissant

The Bed Stuy location's special latte comes with Early grey syrup. I had never heard nor tasted this syrup before so I had to order it. It's so unique I looked all over Tiktok to find the recipe for this syrup!


Rating: 8.5/10



ENLY NYC



Location: 227 Mott St, New York, NY 10012


What I ordered - Tiramisu Latte

This specific drink went viral on Tiktok before Spring break so I had to get it. It had the perfect Asian sweet balance with hint of espresso.

Rating: 9/10



Plantshed at KITH Women



Location: 644 Broadway, New York, NY 10012


What I ordered - Matcha Lavendar Latte and Teddy Bear Financier

The cafe gets all the points for having flowers. They had the cutest Teddy Bear Financier with baked goods.

Rating: 6.5/10



Jabal Coffee House



Location: 1031 Mason St, Dearborn, MI 48124


What I ordered - Jabal Latte and Pistachio Latte

The cafe is always packed and never disappoints. My sister and I always get their Pistachio Latte.

Rating: 9/10



Gateaux Patisserie



Location: 1006 S Military St, Dearborn, MI 48124


What I ordered - Rose Latte and Almond Croissant

The cafe has been my study spot since Sophomore year of college. The drinks and baked goods are always a delicacy!

Rating: 8.5/10



Haraz Coffee House



Location: 32766 Ryan Rd, Warren, MI 48092, USA


What I ordered -Saffron Adeni Latte

This was my Post-Iftar coffee spot during Ramadan.

Rating: 8/10
